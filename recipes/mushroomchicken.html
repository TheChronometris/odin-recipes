Creamy Mushroom Chicken with Crispy Onions

Description

This is a recipe I discovered on BudgetBytes that I found to be absolutely delicious and worth trying at least once. If you're a fan of big umami flavor coupled with succulent chicken and onions on top for that extra added texture, this is a great recipe for you. Ingredients and cooking method will be listed below.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. baby bella mushrooms )
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breast (about 1.3 lbs. total) )
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch pepper )
  • 2 Tbsp butter, divided
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup crispy fried onions
  • 1 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley (optional garnish)
