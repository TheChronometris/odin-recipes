5 Ingredient Turkey Chili

Are you ready for a simple, tasty, and incredibly cheap 5-ingredient chili recipe? Great for those winter days when you want something to warm you up, for game day, or as a bulk meal to last you a few days when you don't feel like cooking, as this is recipe is done in a crock pot/slow cooker. This recipe calls for ground turkey, but you can always substitute your protein of choice. We will be using cans of "Diced Tomato with green chile" as the base of this dish. Use your preferred brand, and you can use the fire roasted variety if you want that extra flavor profile! This is a foundation to add anything you like, especially your own seasonings, so I will not go into much depth on other spices to add other than chili poweder and leave the rest to your imagination/interpretation. Wihtout further delay, the ingredients and instructions are below.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. lean ground turkey (or protein of choice)
  • 3 14.5 oz cans of Diced tomatoes with green chiles
  • 2 cans of beans (These can be black, kidney, pinto, or garbanzo. Can use two different kinds, or 2 cans of one kind)
  • 1 Large white onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp. of chili powder

Steps

  1. Cook the turkey over medium-high heat until browned, about 7-8 minutes, stirring occasionally to evenly cook and break down the meat.
  2. Drain any grease and transfer the ground turkey to your slow cooker. Add the remaining ingredients and stir to combine.
  3. Set the timer for up to 8 hours on low or 4 hours on high.
  4. Serve with your choice of toppings such as cheese, Greek yogurt/sour cream, jalape√±os, chopped cilantro, and tortilla chips and enjoy.
